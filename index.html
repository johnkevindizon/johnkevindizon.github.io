biography

Hi there! I`m John Kevin Dizon. I`m a fingerstyle guitarist from Mabalacat City, Philippines. I started playing guitar way back 2016, I was in 9th grade when our Church pastor invited me to a Youth Camp in Mariveles Bataan. I was spiritually change there when I met Jesus. He found me. I accepted Him as my Lord, God and Savior. When I was there, suddenly, it came to my mind that I want to learn the guitar which I actually learned by His grace and knowledge. So I used my given talent to serve Christ at a local Church at Atlu Bola, Mabalacat City.

The first song I`ve learned is Stay with me by Sam Smith because it`s just have 3 chords. Later on, I learned how to playing basic fingerstyle. The first song I`ve played fingerstyle is All of Me by John Legend. Then I started to arrange my own fingerstyle guitar. My very first arrangement is Torete by Moonstar 88. So I continue learning the guitar, from different styles and techniques, all the way down to Music Theory.

Learning the guitar isn`t that easy, just like any other skills, it requires time, determination, dedication and the most import is your heart, because no matter what you do, if your heart is not in what you are doing, it will be non-sense. Learning the guitar, or should I say loving the music through the guitar is such a priceless thing. By this, I was be able to share my given talent to others, especially to other youths in our church. Above all I thank God, who saved me and making my life in tune.
