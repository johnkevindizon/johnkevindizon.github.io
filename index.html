biography

Welcome to my personal website! I am John Kevin Dizon, a passionate fingerstyle guitarist hailing from the vibrant city of Mabalacat in the beautiful Philippines. My musical journey began in 2016 during my 9th-grade year when a fortuitous invitation to a Youth Camp in Mariveles Bataan changed the course of my life. It was there that I encountered Jesus and experienced a profound spiritual transformation. Embracing Him as my Lord, God, and Savior, I felt a strong calling to explore the world of guitar, an instrument that seemed to resonate with my newfound faith.

My initial steps into the realm of music started with the humble three-chord composition of "Stay with Me" by Sam Smith. Eager to expand my repertoire, I delved into the captivating art of fingerstyle guitar playing, and it was with great excitement that I mastered my first fingerstyle rendition of John Legend's "All of Me." This sparked my creative spirit, leading me to arrange my own fingerstyle adaptations, with my very first endeavor being a heartfelt rendition of "Torete" by Moonstar 88. My journey continued as I delved into various styles, techniques, and even the intricate world of Music Theory.

Learning the guitar proved to be an arduous yet immensely rewarding endeavor. Like any skill worth pursuing, it demanded time, unwavering determination, and unwavering dedication. However, above all else, the most crucial aspect was pouring my heart into every note I played. True passion is the driving force behind meaningful music, as without genuine love and devotion, our efforts would be in vain. This profound connection with the guitar and my deep appreciation for music allowed me to share my God-given talent with others, particularly the vibrant youth community within our local church.

Above all, I am immensely grateful to God for saving me and bringing harmony into my life. It is through Him that I have found purpose, joy, and an unyielding desire to continue honing my craft. Join me on this melodic journey as we celebrate the transformative power of music and embrace the beauty that unfolds when our lives resonate with the rhythms of our hearts.
